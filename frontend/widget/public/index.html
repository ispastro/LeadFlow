🚀 LeadFlow Widget

AI-powered chat widget for lead generation. Click the chat bubble in the bottom right to try it!

📦 Installation

Add these lines to your website:

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://your-widget.vercel.app/widget.css">
<script src="https://your-widget.vercel.app/widget.iife.js"></script>
<script>
  window.LeadFlowWidget.init({
    apiUrl: 'https://leadflow-backend-0457e7580588.herokuapp.com'
  });
</script>

The widget will automatically appear in the bottom right corner of your page.
